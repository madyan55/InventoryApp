# Training Introduction – Madyan Inventory ERP

This system helps you manage products, warehouses, and perform inventory checks professionally.

## Objectives
- Learn how to log in.
- Explore the dashboard.
- Manage products and warehouses.
- Perform inventory checks.
- Review and export reports.

## Basic Steps
1. Log in via LoginActivity.
2. Navigate to DashboardActivity.
3. Manage products and warehouses.
4. Perform inventory checks.
5. Review and export reports.
